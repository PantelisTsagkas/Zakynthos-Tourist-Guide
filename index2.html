üåü Ali's Must-See Island Gems

üèñÔ∏è

Navagio Beach (Shipwreck Beach)

The most famous beach in Greece! Crystal clear turquoise waters and dramatic white cliffs surround a shipwreck from 1980. Accessible only by boat from Porto Vromi or Volimes.

Ali's photo tip: The colors are so incredible, every shot will be Instagram perfect!

Best time: Early morning or late afternoon for fewer crowds

üëÅÔ∏è

Navagio Viewpoint

Breathtaking panoramic views of Shipwreck Beach from above. Perfect for photos and sunset watching. Easy 10-minute walk from the parking area.

Tip: Bring a jacket for evening visits - it gets windy!

üíô

Blue Caves

Stunning natural rock formations with incredible blue illumination created by sunlight reflecting off the white limestone. Take a boat tour from Agios Nikolaos port.

Best time: Morning for the most vivid blue colors

üê¢

Laganas Beach

Famous for Caretta-Caretta sea turtle nesting. Long sandy beach perfect for swimming and sunbathing. Respect turtle nesting areas (May-October).

Turtle watching: Early morning or evening boat tours available

üèõÔ∏è

Zakynthos Town

Charming capital with Venetian architecture, museums, churches, and excellent restaurants. Don't miss Solomos Square and the Byzantine Museum.

Highlights: Church of St. Dionysios, Bohali Hill for sunset views

üåÖ

Kampi Sunset Viewpoint

One of the best sunset spots in Greece! Dramatic clifftop location with tavernas and bars. Arrive early to secure a good spot.

Perfect for: Romantic evenings and stunning photography
