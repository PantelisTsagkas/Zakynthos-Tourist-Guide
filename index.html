Welcome to Zakynthos (Zante)

Zakynthos, the "Flower of the Levant", is a Greek island in the Ionian Sea known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich history. This guide will help you discover its hidden gems and make the most of your holiday.

Must-Visit Places in Zante

Top Pick

Navagio Beach (Shipwreck Beach)

The iconic beach with a shipwreck on white sand, surrounded by towering cliffs. Accessible only by boat, this is Zante's most famous landmark.

Best visited by boat tour

Blue Caves

A series of stunning sea caves where the water glows an incredible azure blue. Perfect for swimming and photography.

Best time to visit: Morning

Keri Caves & Lighthouse

Spectacular cliffs with sea caves and a picturesque lighthouse offering panoramic views of the Ionian Sea.

Great sunset spot

Marathonisi Island (Turtle Island)

A small uninhabited island that's a nesting ground for loggerhead sea turtles. Perfect for a peaceful beach day.

Protected area - be respectful

Porto Limnionas

A hidden gem with crystal clear waters in a natural bay surrounded by rocks. Great for snorkeling and cliff jumping.

Bring snorkeling gear

Zakynthos Town

The island's charming capital with Venetian architecture, museums, and excellent restaurants. Don't miss Solomos Square.

Try local seafood

Essential Travel Tips

Getting Around

  • Rent a car or ATV for maximum flexibility
  • Public buses are available but limited
  • Taxis can be expensive but convenient

Best Time to Visit

  • May-June & September for fewer crowds
  • July-August for vibrant nightlife
  • Water temperature is warmest in August

What to Pack

  • High SPF sunscreen (the sun is strong!)
  • Comfortable walking shoes for exploring
  • Light cover-up for visiting churches

Local Cuisine to Try

  • Moussaka - layered eggplant dish
  • Souvlaki - grilled meat skewers
  • Baklava - sweet pastry with nuts

Money Matters

  • Euro is the local currency
  • Credit cards widely accepted
  • ATMs available in all major areas

Cultural Tips

  • Greet with "Yasas" (hello)
  • Tipping 5-10% is appreciated
  • Dress modestly when visiting churches
